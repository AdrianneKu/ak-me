About

I am a digital designer for hire. Passionate about clean nice-looking design, obsessed about the fine details and always eager to take on a challenge.

I am a highly creative and multi-talented designer with experience in print production, layout & design and front-end web development.

Taking projects from start to finish, I produce compliant standard websites and design files for print.

I am knowledgeable in CSS and HTML with some experience in jQuery and Flash.

When I'm not in front of my computer designing, I am in front of my computer learning and improving my skills as a web designer.
